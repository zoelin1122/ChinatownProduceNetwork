##_HERE IS MY DATA


It is important for me to make data I created during this project to be open source and downloadable by everyone to promotes transparency, collaboration, and innovation.

I hope that this data can facilitate collaboration among researchers, policymakers, and community members, leading to more robust analyses and more effective solutions to problems. I hope people will build upon existing data and develop new tools and applications.

It would be wonderful if more people can contribute to the analysis and interpretation of the data, and find new insights, perspectives, and approaches to understanding the complex dynamics of Chinatown's produce market.
